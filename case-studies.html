Healing Hands by Santa · Laguna Niguel, CA · 2025

The problem, in her words

"I'm hands-on with a client for 60–90 minutes at a time. My phone rings, I can't answer it. By the time I'm done, they've called someone else. I was losing bookings I never even knew about."

— Santa E., Licensed Massage Therapist, Laguna Niguel, CA

Santa ran a solo practice. She was booked solid, which meant every call that came in during a session went to voicemail. Most callers didn't leave a message. The ones who did, she'd call back hours later. Half didn't pick up. She had no idea how much she was losing.

What we installed

Setup took one afternoon. We connected her existing business number and configured three things:

1

AI answering on the first ring. Every call picked up instantly, introduced as Santa's front desk, and handled with her service menu and pricing.

2

Automatic booking via text follow-up. If a caller couldn't book during the call, they received a text link within 90 seconds with her availability.

3

Smart call logs with caller intent. Every call summarized by topic (new client, reschedule, price question) so Santa could review between sessions without listening to voicemails.

The first missed call

The day after go-live, Santa was mid-session when her phone rang. Before, it would have rung out. This time, the AI answered. When she finished with her client and checked her dashboard, she saw the summary:

Incoming text: automated follow-up

Hi, it's Santa's scheduling team. We just missed your call, so sorry! I'd love to get you booked. Here's a link to grab a time that works for you: [booking link]. If you have questions, just reply here.

Sent 38 seconds after the call ended. The caller booked a 90-minute session that afternoon.

That one booking was $130. She never would have known the call came in.

The first fourteen days

23 calls answered that previously would have gone unanswered or to voicemail.

9 new bookings directly attributed to calls that were caught and followed up on.

4 rebookings from clients who had stopped calling after being ignored, reached back through the follow-up flow.

$1,060 recovered by day 14. More than double the monthly subscription cost.

The thirty-day result

By the end of month one, Santa had recovered $2,560 in revenue that would have been permanently lost. Her booking rate from inbound calls went from an estimated 30% (the calls she could actually pick up) to 82%.

She also gained something harder to measure: she stopped worrying between sessions. The phone rings, and she knows it's handled.

What made the recovery possible

Four components of the Ops by Noell system worked together:

Noell Front Desk

Live AI answering on every inbound call, trained on Santa's voice, services, and pricing.

Missed Call Text-Back

Automated follow-up texts sent within 90 seconds of any unanswered call, with her booking link included.

Smart Call Insights

Every call logged and summarized by intent: new client, rescheduling, cancellation. Nothing slips.

Noell Care (ongoing management)

System tuned continuously: updated scripts, adjusted follow-up timing, added holiday hours, handled edge cases.

What Santa kept

Everything she valued about her practice stayed intact. She still does intake herself for new clients. She still chooses which calls to return personally. She didn't hand over her business. She filled the gap that was silently bleeding it.

"I kept waiting for it to feel weird or impersonal. It doesn't. My clients just think I got really good at texting back."

What this proves

Solo service providers don't need more availability. They need their existing demand to stop leaking. Santa wasn't short on clients. She was short on a system to hold onto them while she worked.

$2,560 in 30 days isn't a fluke. It's what happens when every call gets answered, every caller gets a follow-up, and nothing falls through the gap between sessions.
