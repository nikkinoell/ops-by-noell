Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What are cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences and understand how visitors use the site.

How we use cookies

The Ops by Noell website uses a minimal set of cookies:

Essential cookies

These are required for the site to function. They enable things like the booking form and navigation. You cannot opt out of essential cookies.

Analytics cookies

We use standard web analytics to understand aggregate page traffic — which pages are visited, where visitors come from, and how long they stay. This data is anonymized. We do not use session recordings, heatmaps, or fingerprinting tools.

Third-party embeds

Our booking calendar is provided by a third-party scheduling platform (GoHighLevel). When you interact with the booking widget, that platform may set its own cookies subject to its own privacy policy. We do not control those cookies.

What we do not do

  • We do not use advertising or retargeting cookies
  • We do not share cookie data with ad networks
  • We do not track you across other websites

Managing cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect how certain parts of the site function. Instructions for common browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our services evolve. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
