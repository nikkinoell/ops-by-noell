The AI front desk market has grown fast. Here's how to think through your options:

  • Self-serve platforms (My AI Front Desk, VAPI, etc.): Low cost, high time investment. Good if you have a technical team and time to configure and maintain.
  • Live answering services (Ruby, etc.): Warm, human feel. Limited hours, no booking, high cost at volume.
  • Messaging platforms (Podium, Weave, Birdeye): Good for existing-client communication. Not built for new-client recovery or front desk replacement.
  • Done-for-you (Ops by Noell): Highest leverage if you want it built, installed, and running without your involvement. Flat rate, managed end-to-end.

The right answer depends on how much time you have and how much you want to manage. If the answer is "none," that's what Ops by Noell is built for.
